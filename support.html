Frequently Asked Questions

How does TripTrail automatically track my trips?

TripTrail uses your device's location data to detect significant changes in your whereabouts. This allows the app to automatically record and display the countries you visit on your travel profile.

Is my location data private?

Yes, your location data is used solely to enhance your travel experience by automatically updating your trip information. It is not shared with third parties and will only be used on your device.

What if I grant limited access to my photos?

If you choose limited access, you can manually select additional photos for TripTrail to scan. You will be able to select more photos. If you want to provide access to your whole photo library, you can change the access permission in the iOS settings.

How can I add the TripTrail widget?

On your iPhone, long-press the home screen, tap the '+' icon, and search for "TripTrail". Then add the widget to your home screen for quick access to your travel stats.

How to Use TripTrail

Automatic Trip Tracking: TripTrail continuously monitors your location for significant changes and automatically updates your travel profile with the countries you visit.

Photo Scanning: The app scans the location metadata embedded in your photos to further enrich your travel history.

Statistics & Maps: Explore your travel history with interactive maps and detailed statistics for a comprehensive overview of your journeys.

Widget: Easily add the TripTrail widget to your home screen to view your latest travel stats at a glance.

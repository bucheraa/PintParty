Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to our App (“Pint Party”), a drinking-game experience designed solely for users who have reached the legal drinking age in their respective jurisdictions. In the United States, the minimum age is 21. If you are under 21 in the U.S. (or under 18 in jurisdictions where 18 is the legal drinking age), do not use this App.

By downloading or using the App, you acknowledge that you meet the minimum legal drinking age and agree to this Privacy Policy, as well as any Terms and Conditions governing your use of the App. If you do not agree, please discontinue use immediately.

2. No Personal Data Collection

No Off-Device Transmission: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data (e.g., name, email, IP addresses, or other identifying information) from your device to any remote servers or third parties.

Local Data Only: Any user-generated content, game settings, or scores remain entirely on your device. We do not access or process these data points.

No Analytics / Tracking: We do not use analytics tools, cookies, web beacons, or other tracking technologies within the App or on our Support Website to monitor user behavior.

3. User Age and Eligibility

Age Restriction: You must be at least 21 years old if you reside in the United States (or at least 18 in other jurisdictions, if that is the legal drinking age) to access or use this App.

Age Verification: By using this App, you confirm you are of legal drinking age. We do not collect date-of-birth information or other personal details, so you are responsible for complying with the legal drinking age requirements in your location.

Prohibited Use by Minors: If we discover or have reason to believe that you are under the legal drinking age, we reserve the right to restrict or terminate your access to the App without notice.
